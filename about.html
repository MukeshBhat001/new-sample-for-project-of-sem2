About Nepal

Discover the rich history, culture, and geography of Nepal

Geography & Location

Nepal is a landlocked country in South Asia, located between China and India. It covers an area of 147,516 square kilometers and is home to eight of the world's fourteen highest peaks, including Mount Everest.

Landlocked 147,516 km¬≤ 8 Highest Peaks

Culture & People

Nepal is incredibly diverse with over 125 ethnic groups and 123 languages. The majority practice Hinduism and Buddhism. The country is known for its warm hospitality, rich traditions, and colorful festivals like Dashain and Tihar.

125 Ethnic Groups 123 Languages Hindu-Buddhist

History & Heritage

Nepal has a rich history dating back thousands of years. It was never colonized and has maintained its independence throughout history. The country is home to 10 UNESCO World Heritage Sites, showcasing its architectural and cultural significance.

Hiking Places in Nepal

Discover world-class trekking routes in the Himalayas

Everest Base Camp Trek

The world's most famous trek takes you to the base of Mount Everest at 5,364m. Experience Sherpa culture, visit ancient monasteries, and witness breathtaking Himalayan views. Duration: 12-14 days.

Challenging 14 Days 5,364m Sherpa Culture
Learn More

Annapurna Circuit Trek

One of Nepal's classic treks offering diverse landscapes from subtropical forests to alpine meadows. Cross the Thorong La Pass at 5,416m and experience varied cultures. Duration: 15-20 days.

Moderate-Hard 15-20 Days 5,416m Diverse Landscape
Learn More

Langtang Valley Trek

Known as the "Valley of Glaciers," this trek offers stunning mountain views, rhododendron forests, and Tamang culture. Less crowded than other popular treks. Duration: 7-10 days.

Moderate 7-10 Days 4,984m Less Crowded

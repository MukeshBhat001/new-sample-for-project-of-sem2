Frequently Asked Questions

What is the best time to visit Nepal?

The best time to visit Nepal is during autumn (October-November) and spring (March-May) when the weather is clear and temperatures are moderate. These seasons offer the best mountain views and comfortable trekking conditions.

Do I need a visa to visit Nepal?

Most visitors need a visa to enter Nepal. Tourist visas can be obtained on arrival at the airport or at border crossings. We can assist you with visa information and requirements.

What should I pack for trekking?

Essential items include warm clothing, good hiking boots, sleeping bag, first aid kit, and proper trekking gear. The specific items depend on the season and trek difficulty. We provide detailed packing lists for each trek.

Is it safe to travel in Nepal?

Nepal is generally safe for tourists. However, it's important to follow safety guidelines, especially when trekking in remote areas. We provide experienced guides and comprehensive safety measures for all our tours.

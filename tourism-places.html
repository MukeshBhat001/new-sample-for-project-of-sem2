Tourism Places in Nepal

Explore the cultural and natural wonders of Nepal

Kathmandu

The capital city of Nepal, Kathmandu is a vibrant blend of ancient culture and modern life. Home to seven UNESCO World Heritage Sites, including Kathmandu Durbar Square, Swayambhunath (Monkey Temple), and Boudhanath Stupa.

UNESCO Sites Cultural Heritage Temples Museums
Explore Kathmandu

Pokhara

Known as the "City of Lakes," Pokhara is famous for its stunning natural beauty. Phewa Lake, Davis Falls, and the World Peace Pagoda are major attractions. It's also the gateway to the Annapurna region.

Lakes Mountain Views Adventure Sports Peaceful
Explore Pokhara

Chitwan National Park

Nepal's first national park and a UNESCO World Heritage Site. Home to the endangered one-horned rhinoceros, Bengal tigers, and over 500 bird species. Offers jungle safaris, elephant rides, and cultural experiences.

Wildlife Safari UNESCO Site Nature
Explore Chitwan

Lumbini

The sacred birthplace of Lord Buddha and a UNESCO World Heritage Site. Features the Maya Devi Temple, Ashoka Pillar, and monasteries built by various countries representing different Buddhist traditions.

UNESCO Site Buddhist Pilgrimage Sacred Birthplace Spiritual
Explore Lumbini
